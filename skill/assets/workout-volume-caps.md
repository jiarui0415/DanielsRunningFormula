# Workout Volume Caps (Per Jack Daniels)

Source: Jack Daniels' Running Formula (4th ed., 2022), Chapter 4

These caps apply **every session, every week**. Check each quality workout against the runner's weekly mileage before prescribing.

---

## Per-session maximums

| Session type | Cap 1 | Cap 2 | Use whichever is less |
|-------------|-------|-------|----------------------|
| **Long run (L)** | 30% of weekly mileage (if <40 mi/wk) | 150 minutes | — |
| **Long run (L)** | 25% of weekly mileage (if ≥40 mi/wk) | 150 minutes | Whichever is less |
| **Marathon-pace (M)** | 20% of weekly mileage | 18 miles / 110 minutes | Whichever is less |
| **Threshold (T)** | 10% of weekly mileage | 30 minutes (steady tempo) | Whichever is less |
| **Interval (I)** | 8% of weekly mileage | ~10K total (per session) | Whichever is less |
| **Repetition (R)** | 5% of weekly mileage | 5 miles total (per session) | Whichever is less |

---

## Quick-reference by mileage tier

| Weekly mileage | Max L run | Max M session | Max T session | Max I session | Max R session |
|---------------|-----------|---------------|---------------|---------------|---------------|
| 25 mi/wk | 7.5 mi or 150 min | 5 mi | 2.5 mi / ~15 min | 2 mi | 1.25 mi |
| 30 mi/wk | 9 mi or 150 min | 6 mi | 3 mi / 18 min | 2.4 mi | 1.5 mi |
| 40 mi/wk | 10 mi or 150 min | 8 mi | 4 mi / 24 min | 3.2 mi | 2 mi |
| 50 mi/wk | 12.5 mi or 150 min | 10 mi | 5 mi / 30 min | 4 mi | 2.5 mi |
| 60 mi/wk | 15 mi or 150 min | 12 mi / 110 min | 6 mi / 30 min | 4.8 mi | 3 mi |
| 70 mi/wk | 17.5 mi or 150 min | 14 mi / 110 min | 7 mi / 30 min | 5.6 mi | 3.5 mi |
| 85 mi/wk | 21 mi or 150 min | 17 mi / 110 min | 8.5 mi / 30 min | 6.8 mi | 4.25 mi |
| 100 mi/wk | 25 mi or 150 min | 18 mi / 110 min | 10 mi / 30 min | 8 mi | 5 mi |

**T cap note:** For cruise intervals (not steady tempo), 30-minute cap applies to total T-pace running time, excluding rest intervals.

**I cap note:** At I pace, 5 minutes is the maximum duration for a *single* work bout. For example, at VDOT 60, I pace is ~3:04/800m — a runner can do 800m I reps but not mile I reps (which would take ~6 min at that VDOT).

**R cap note:** At R pace, 2 minutes is the maximum duration for a *single* work bout. For example, at VDOT 60, R pace is 75 sec/400m — 400m R reps are fine; 800m R reps require VDOT ≥77.

## Training stress points (for total weekly load check)

Jack's stress-point system helps ensure total weekly load isn't excessive:

| Intensity | Points per minute |
|-----------|-----------------|
| E | 0.2 pts/min |
| M | 0.4 pts/min |
| T | 0.6 pts/min |
| I | 1.0 pts/min |
| R | 1.5 pts/min |

No explicit weekly maximum given, but use this to compare weeks and avoid large spikes.
