## Primary Source

**Jack Daniels' Running Formula, 4th Edition (2022)**
Author: Jack Daniels, Ph.D.
Publisher: Human Kinetics
ISBN: 978-1492592020

This skill implements Jack Daniels' VDOT training methodology, including:
- VDOT tables (Tables 5.1, 5.2, 5.3) — Chapter 5
- Training types and intensities (E/M/T/I/R) — Chapter 4
- Phase structure (I/II/III/IV) — Chapter 10
- VDOT decay during training breaks (Table 9.1) — Chapter 9
- No-equipment strength circuit — Chapter 9
- Race-specific plans — Chapters 13, 15, 16

VDOT tables were originally developed by Jack Daniels and Jimmy Gilbert, published in *Oxygen Power* (1979) and refined through subsequent editions of this book.
